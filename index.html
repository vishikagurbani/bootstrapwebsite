    About Us

    Founded in 1974, we have been pioneers in introducing Welded Wiremesh in the country. We are one of the oldest manufacturers,and through our quality and customer service, we have created a niche in the field of manufacturing of Welded Wiremesh.

    Our Plant

    We have three fully automated and sophisticated plants for manufacturing Welded Wiremesh with highest quality, accuracy and with the widest range. The plants are also equipped with latest machines for making Chainlink Fencing & Hexagonal Wirenetting.

    Wire Mesh Perfection

    Welded Wiremesh or weldmesh is manufactured on our Fully Automatic Electronic Welder by Electric Resistance Welding. A series of parallal longitudnal wires with required spacing are welded with cross wires at the intersection points as per required spacing. All the spacings between wires are controlled by an automatic programmed mechanism of highest reliability.

    Customer Relations

    The name Modern is synonymous for quality and customer satisfaction. We have a large network of Customers & Dealers with whom we have cultivated and nurtured very long standing relationships.
